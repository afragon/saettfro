BOG Design

BOG Design is a Gothenburg, Sweden, based startup within interior and exterior design. A common passion for art, light and design brought the founders Nikola Matakovic and Afra Noubarzadeh together.

The project started in the beginning of December as both members, currently attending Gothenburg School of Art, decided to join forces and establish a common platform for creative exploration. One month later the first product, The Zeppelin Lamp was ready and BOG Design was formed.

Knowel // 2012 - 2014

Project Cordinator

Knowel is a non-profit organization based in Gothenburg, Sweden, run by master students from Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship. Knowel’s mantra is spreading vital knowledge, and this is done through the different projects that each team undertakes. Knowel is not a charity organization, rather we are a social entrepreneurial venture that aims to spread the knowledge needed to build a better society.

With the experience from being part of the Tanzania 2013 project I continued the following year with Knowel as a project coordinator, where I managed five international and local projects. My responsiblities covered communication within teams and cross projects.

Leftover Family // 2006 - 2012

Leftover Family was a creative collective with deep roots in youth culture, mostly Hip Hop. It consisted of musicians, Scandinavian breakdance champions, photographers and artists. The main purpose was to establish a united platform for emerging artist to collaborate cross art mediums. The greatest achivement by the collective was the club "Västkust Rockers". In 2010 it was rewarded as the best club in Gothenburg, Sweden.

I was the founder of the collective and I mainly expressed myself through clothing, prints and tattooing.
